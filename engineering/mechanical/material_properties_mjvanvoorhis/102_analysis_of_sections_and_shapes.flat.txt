<p><pre>
Mass Properties of Shapes
Nomenclature:
<br>rho = density, weight/unit volume 
<br>m = mass
<br>I = mass moment of inertia, 
<br>g = acceleration of gravity, = length/time^2 when density is given as 
force/unit volume (ie. weight/unit volume). 
</pre></p>

<hr  size=1>

<p><pre>
<img alt="Rod Illustration" src="taba3101.gif">
Rod relationships:
------------------
m = (3.14159 / 4)  *( d^2 * l) * (rho / g) => See Nomenclature Above
Iy = m * l^2 / 12
Iz = m * l^2 / 12
Ix = m * d^2 / 8
</pre></p>

<p><pre>
<img alt="Round disk Illustration" src="taba3102.gif">
Round disk relationships:
-------------------------
m = (3.14159 / 4) * (d^2 * t) * (rho / g) => See Nomenclature Above
Iy = m * d^2 / 16
Iz = m * d^2 / 16
Ix = m * d^2 / 8
</pre></p>

<p><pre>
<img alt="Rectangular prism Illustration" src="taba3103.gif">
Rectangular prism relationships:
--------------------------------
m = (a * b * c) * (rho / g) => See Nomenclature Above
Iy = m / 12 * (a^2 + c^2)
Iz = m / 12 * (b^2 + c^2)
Ix = m / 12 * (a^2 + b^2)
</pre>
</pre></p>

<p><pre>
<img alt="Cylinder Illustration" src="taba3104.gif">
Cylinder relationships:
-----------------------
m = (3.14159 /4) * (d^2 * l) * (rho / g) => See Nomenclature Above
Iy = m / 48 * (3 * d^2 + 4 * l^2)
Iz = m / 48 * (3 * d^2 + 4 * l^2)
Ix = m * d^2 / 8
</pre></p>

<p><pre>
<img alt="Hollow Cylinder Illustration" src="taba3105.gif">
Hollow Cylinder relationships:
------------------------------
m = (3.14159 / 4) * ((d0^2 - di^2)*L) * (rho/g) => See Nomenclature Above
Iy = m / 48 * (3 * d0^2 + 3 * di^2 + 4 * l^2)
Iz = m / 48 * (3 * d0^2 + 3 * di^2 + 4 * l^2)
Ix = m / 8 * (d0^2 + di^2)
</pre></p>
